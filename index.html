Welcome!

This is a dashboard intended for off-site nurses monitoring their patient populations. The dashboard is intended to feed information in from electronic medical records (EMRs) and from apps used by patients to track doses and post complaints or status updates. The nurse can use the checkboxes ( ) to check off any updates and signify her acknowledgment of the information.

The dashboard includes a Patient List for viewing the entire population sorted by level of severity, which is indicated by a small bullet. After interviewing a few physicians, a color system was created, ranking severity by Critical (), Guarded (), and Stable ().

The first patient, Jackie C., is clickable and is an demo of the functionality of pulling up a patient profile. Her full name and and the ability to contact her is included in her profile.
